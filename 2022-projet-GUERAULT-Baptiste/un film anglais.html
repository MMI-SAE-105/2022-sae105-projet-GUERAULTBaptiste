An emblematic film Ratatouille

-Ratatouille is a movie released in theaters in 2007 on August 1 (June 22, 2007 in the USA), directed by brad bird and co-founder Jan pinkava. It is the eighth CGI animated film from Pixar Studios in the United States.

-The film tells the adventures and adventures of Remy a young rat who dreams of becoming a great French chef. The opposition of his family or the fact of being a rat in a job that hates them does not discourage him at all. He is ready to do anything to make his passion his daily life.

-The young rat who has been separated from his family following a disaster that they tried to escape with his colony, then dislodged from his place of settlement, then settles in the kitchen "chez gusteau" (or rather in the sewers) which is a great Parisian restaurant. So great is the temptation to venture into this almost impossible challenge and especially by adding the dangers and traps it makes the task even more difficult.

-Separated by the gap between passion and dreams and then the opposite side which is his condition of rat hater by the job he will discover the true meaning of adventure, friendship and family. He will have to find the courage to become a great chef and will meet a young kitchen assistant named Alfredo Linguini who may help him to achieve his dream.
