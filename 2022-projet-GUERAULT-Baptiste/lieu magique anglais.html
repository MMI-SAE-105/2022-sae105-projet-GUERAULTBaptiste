Paris a magical place

-Paris is the capital of France, the city is divided into 20 districts, Paris is located in the center of the Paris basin on a loop of the Seine between the confluence of the Marne and Oise rivers. The city "exists" since the 3rd century by the Gauls. The old name of Paris was Lutetia taken around the year 310 and developed by successive enclosures.

Paris becomes in the 10th century one of the main cities with several buildings like abbeys, cathedrals and royal palaces. With the university of Paris in the 12th century.

-Its economic and political importance continues to grow, the city of Paris becomes one of the most important in the Christian world. In the 19th century, it was the capital first of the Napoleonic Empire and then of arts and pleasures. In the 20th and 21st centuries, it became one of the main cities of the European Union. Since the Middle Ages, it has played a leading role in the world in terms of its activity and influence.
